# Privacy Policy
**Effective Date: July 17, 2026**

Welcome to PitchPro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

### 1. Information We Collect
We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as data generated automatically through your use of PitchPro:
* **Account Information:** Name, email address, and authentication credentials (managed securely via our database providers).
* **User Content:** The text, prompts, company details, target customer profiles (ICPs), and files you input to generate pitch decks or search for leads.
* **Usage Data:** Diagnostic data, error logs, and metrics on how you interact with our platform.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for the following purposes:
* To generate, personalize, and export your AI pitch decks.
* To facilitate our Lead Generation features by translating your Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) into search parameters.
* To maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the functionality of our website.
* To communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, and account support.

### 3. Third-Party Integrations & AI Processing
PitchPro relies on trusted third-party services to deliver core functionalities. By using our platform, you acknowledge that certain data will be securely processed by these providers:
* **AI Models:** Your text inputs are processed by third-party Large Language Models (such as Google Gemini) to generate slide content and strategic insights. These models do not use your private data to train their public foundation models.
* **Database & Hosting:** Your account data and saved pitch decks are stored securely using modern cloud infrastructure (such as Supabase and Vercel).
* **Lead Generation Partners:** If you utilize our lead generation features, your search parameters may be securely passed to integrated data partners (such as Clay, Apollo, or RapidAPI) strictly for the purpose of retrieving your requested contact lists.

### 4. Data Security & Retention
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We only retain your personal information and generated content for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
